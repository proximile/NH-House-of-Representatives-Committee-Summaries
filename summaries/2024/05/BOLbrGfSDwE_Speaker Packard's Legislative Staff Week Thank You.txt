During a New Hampshire House of Representatives meeting, a representative paused before proceeding to the third reading of a motion to acknowledge **Legislative Staff Week**. The representative expressed gratitude to the **general court staff** for their essential contributions to the day-to-day operations of the House, emphasizing their dedication and commitment. They praised the staff as **"the best staff in the nation"** and called for applause in their honor. Following this acknowledgment, the chair recognized **Representative Osborne from Auburn** to proceed with the third reading.
